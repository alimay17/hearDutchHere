    • Possessives
      In Dutch, you can add an S to names for the possessive - but only with people's names. Use 'van' (of) or 'zijn' (his, its) or 'haar' (her, its) in other cases
      'Major', 'Important', 'Serious' There is no Dutch equivalent for English 'major.' So I wrote a chapter about 'voornaamste'/'belangrijkste' 'most important' and 'belangrijk'' 'important' - and 'serieus' and 'ernstig' 'serious, grave'
